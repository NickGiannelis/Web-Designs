Traditional Greek Tavern Website

I have designed and implemented "Mouries," a web portfolio showcasing a traditional Greek taverna. It highlights the authentic dishes and warm atmosphere of our establishment. This includes the creation of a menu featuring classic Greek recipes such as moussaka and souvlaki, and the development of an inviting environment through a carefully crafted gallery and navigation experience.

Greek Pastry Shop Website

I have created this website for Birbas, a pastry shop consisting of 5 pages, with categorized products and Google reviews. The website is also fully mobile responsive. Birbas stands out for its sweet creations, made with pure ingredients. It offers traditional treats like baklava and walnut cake, as well as contemporary recipes to satisfy every taste. Visit to enjoy freshly made delights and experience live baking events and pastry workshops, where you can learn the art of pastry-making while enjoying delicious sweets.

Greek Grill House Website

This webpage is for Perivola, a traditional grill house that offers a unique gastronomic experience. I have also written the content for the shop's history and implemented an automation feature that allows customers to share content from social media (e.g., Facebook). When a new event is posted on Facebook, it is automatically added to the New Events page on the website, ensuring the latest events are always updated. Perivola serves delicious skewers, souvlaki, and steaks made with fresh, high-quality ingredients. The aroma of perfectly grilled meat and the warm atmosphere guarantee a memorable dining experience. In addition to its exquisite cuisine, Perivola hosts live events, adding excitement and entertainment to every visit, making each one unforgettable.
